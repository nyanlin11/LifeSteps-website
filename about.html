Aim

LifeSteps is to help kids develop the life skills they need to succeed. We aim to teach important skills like confidence, emotional understanding, and practical habits that prepare them for real-life challenges. Our goal is to make learning these skills simple, fun, and meaningful for every child.

Nyan Lin Zay

Hi, I’m Nyan Lin Zay, the creator of LifeSteps. I’m passionate about helping kids learn the skills they need to succeed in life. Growing up, I realized how important it is to build confidence, manage emotions, and develop practical habits. That’s why I created this website—to share helpful tips and tools that can make a difference. I hope you find this space inspiring and useful for you and the kids in your life!
