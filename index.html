What are life skills?

Life skills are essential abilities that help children become confident, responsible, and independent adults. These skills go beyond academics, covering areas like self-care, problem-solving, communication, decision-making, and emotional regulation. By learning these skills early, kids can build a strong foundation for success and healthy relationships.
